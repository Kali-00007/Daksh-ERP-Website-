Privacy Policy

Your business data belongs to you

This page explains the privacy approach for the Daksh ERP website and product information pages.

1. Website Purpose

This website is intended to provide information about Daksh ERP, product features, documentation, downloads, release notes and support contact options.

2. Business Data

Daksh ERP follows an offline-first product approach. Business records created inside the installed software are intended to remain within the user's installed environment unless the user chooses to share or back them up externally.

3. Website Data Collection

This static website does not require visitors to create an online account. If contact forms or third-party services are added later, any submitted information may be used for support, demonstration, licensing or business communication.

4. Contact Information

If a user voluntarily shares contact details such as name, email, phone number or business inquiry, that information may be used to respond to the request.

5. Downloads and Updates

The website may provide installer files, update packages and version information. Users should download software only from official sources.

6. Local Software Data

The installed Daksh ERP software may store business data locally on the user's system. Users are responsible for protecting their systems, maintaining backups and controlling access to their business environment.

7. Third-Party Services

If analytics, forms, hosting, email or support tools are added later, those services may process limited information according to their own policies.

8. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the website, software and support channels evolve.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact details provided on the Contact page.

Contact support or review the documentation for product details.
